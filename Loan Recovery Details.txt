### Task 1: Exploratory Analysis --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Initially I created buckets based on the historical data which was 'previous payment history' but upon further research got to know something interesting about the Days_Past_Due. There's a rule about DPDs, according to which, a loan is classified as NPA once it has been overdue for more than 90 days. So, I thought why not do the segmentation based on DPDs and hence I created three buckets based on DPDs : High, Medium and Low, and guess what even the accounts which were considered as GOOD in the past were in LOW probability zone, which required aggressive actions to recover.


Hence, for a basic understanding of the data, I created a Dashboard in PowerBI which shows the accounts segmentation based on DPDs and other key factors being used as filters. A card visual to show the total outstanding amount and another card visual for displaying the recovery rate.

KEY INSIGHTS FROM THE DASHBOARD :

1. 71% accounts of the 10k accounts comes under the low probability zone, which means they require aggressive actions both legal and field visits.
2. Only 7% accounts of the 10k accounts are highly recoverable which could most probably be recovered with SMS/ WhatsApp/ calls as soft reminders.
3. The 21% of accounts in the medium bucket needs immediate consideration as well, as they may get converted to low probability zone.
4. One more important thing to notice, even the accounts which were marked as GOOD (2918 accounts), 71% of these accounts are in low recovery probability zone which again needs aggressive actions.
5. Overall the outstanding amount comes around to be 866M with 13% recovery rate.


### Task 2: Strategy Design ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

----- Account Prioritization & assigning the most effective channel.

As per the Exploratory Analysis Done, It's clear that 71% of the accounts are in low recovery probability zone which also means that these accounts have DPD more than 90 and reaching towards the count of 300.

1. Account Prioritization.

--HIGH PRIORITY : Accounts With High DPD count (90+) & High Outstanding Amount because of High Risk + High money to be recovered.
--MEDIUM PRIORITY : Accounts in the medium recovery probability zone (31-90 Days) to avoid them getting converted to NPAs.
--LOW PRIORITY : Accounts in the high recovery probability zone(1-30 days) recoverable with soft nudges like SMS/ whatsapp

2. Channel Assignment.

-- The Three Segments are as follows :

High Recovery Probability (1 - 30 Days) --> channel :  SMS / WhatsApp / Regular Follow-up
Medium Recovery Probability (31 - 90 Days) --> channel : Call / Field Visit to avoid them getting converted to NPAs.
Low Recovery Probability (90+ Days) --> channel : aggressive action : Field visit/ Legal action

If last_contact_outcome : No Response : call / SMS / WhatsApp / Regular Follow-up
                          Paid : Low Priority/ close
                          Promised : call / Field visit
                          Refused : Field Visit / Legal Action (Aggressive)

If previous_payment_history : Good : approach can be much softer
                              Poor : Firmer and assertive approach earlier

## Resource Optimization
Agents being limited and legal actions being costly, hence the resources need to be utilized only for Accounts With High DPD count (90+), High Outstanding Amount and where the attempt to contact has failed or has been refused by the borrower.

### Task 4: Business Impact ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Analysis reveals that most of the outstanding money is held in the Medium Recovery segment and hence it holds the highest potential. On the other hand the low recovery segment holds the highest NPAs with a recovery rate of 4.5% only. Both the segments are high priority ones, with the low recovery segment needing aggressive legal actions and field visits especially the accounts with last_contact_outcome as refused and no response. And for the accounts with status as "Promised" should be led with consistent call follow-ups.

2. When it comes to the resource optimization for the High Priority accounts as mentioned above, the accounts can be filtered to the last_contact_outcome status as "Refused" and "No Response" and only for such high-value accounts the legal actions and field visits should be reserved. For other last_contact_outcome statuses, the primary channels should be calls/SMS/WhatsApp.

3. As per the analysis, Medium Recovery and Low Recovery segments have the recovery rate as 13% and 4.5% respectively. The recovery rate can be improvised by converting the Medium Recovery accounts to High Recovery ones before they become Low Recovery accounts. And by taking intense actions (legal/ field visits) for the low recovery accounts.

4. Overall the Scoring logic (DPD + outcome + history + income) led to the segmentation of accounts based on four effective factors rather than just relying on DPD, eventually leading to more accurate segmentation and better decision making.
